Build TryBe

Build Trybe is a community of mentors, instructors, and volunteers that empower youth with employable skills. We are a bridge connecting youth to opportunity. Build Trybe is a proud program of Cornerstones of Care.

Every dollar donated goes towards our mission of helping youth in foster care thrive. Your donation ensures we can continue to do our important work. From our outdoor education centers to our woodshop to our metal shop and beyond, your support helps youth throughout KC and beyond.
